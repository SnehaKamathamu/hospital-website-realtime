About Us

Dr. Ravi’s Poly Clinic was started in 2021 with the intent to offer accessible and compassionate healthcare to the local community. Led by Dr.C.Ravidhar, a pediatrician with over 25 years of experience in treating children, he pursued his MBBS from Kakatiya Medical College and Gandhi Medical College, and later completed his Diploma in Child Health (DCH) from Niloufer Hospital. Conveniently located near Starlite Pavilion in Chinthal, the clinic offers easy access to patients from nearby communities. Alongside pediatric care, the clinic also provides specialized expertise in gynecology and general medicine, supported by a team of dedicated and qualified doctors. At Dr. Ravi’s Poly Clinic, we are committed to delivering compassionate, high-quality healthcare while ensuring every patient is treated with kindness, respect, and individual attention.
